Why do we do it?

The term UX Angel is inspired by Trail Angel, a name given to those who dedicate time and energy every year to support those hiking 2663 mi (4286 km) from Mexico to Canada on the Pacific Crest Trail. Like startups, only a small percentage of hikers succeed in their journey. It is UX Angels’ belief that startup innovation fuels the global economy and improves our daily lives. We dedicate our own resources and expertise to projects we recognize as pioneers for a better future.

The reality is, good ideas are better with great design. Better design means faster and more effective communication. The effectiveness of design can and should be measured by how quickly and accurately it communicates it's intended message. The line between art and design in our experience has been the level of accuracy required in that communication.

Human beings are phenomenally complicated, each individual perceives and interprets their surroundings differently. The task of a designer is to create an experience which rises above these differences. An experience that can connect with us deeply, and quickly, but with the generality to reach minds of all kinds.
